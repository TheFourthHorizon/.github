- A powerful and illuminating dive into one of the world’s most influential yet often overlooked faiths. This [episode](https://youtu.be/-qrZC0x4M44) brilliantly traces Zoroastrianism’s groundbreaking vision—from Zarathustra’s call to moral responsibility and cosmic truth, to a universe shaped by free will, justice, and the battle between light and darkness. Thought-provoking, historically rich, and deeply relevant for understanding the moral architecture of the modern spiritual world—another compelling chapter in a truly exceptional [series](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4ZnjqJ5pyZS3lFouw8LkrxlrmbIRUNr6).
